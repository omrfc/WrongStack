feat(autonomous-coordinator): wire run() startup to TUI and slash context

Adds onCoordinatorStart/onCoordinatorStop callbacks to SlashCommandContext
and RunTuiOptions, enabling TUI and slash commands to start/stop the
AutonomousCoordinator loop. The coordinator's run() is now callable
fire-and-forget via onCoordinatorStart(goal), and stop() via
onCoordinatorStop(). This completes the integration of AutonomousCoordinator
with the existing autonomy infrastructure.
